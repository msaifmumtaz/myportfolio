A swimming tracking system that analyzes video footage to track swimmers and provide performance metrics. Users can upload videos or connect a real-time feed to monitor races and training sessions.

### Technology Used
- **Python**: Core processing language.
- **OpenCV**: Computer vision library for tracking and detection.
- **Cloud**: Scalable infrastructure for video processing.
- **Admin Dashboard**: PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript interface.

### Key Features
- **Swimmer Tracking**: Identifies and tracks swimmers in the pool.
- **Race Analytics**: Determines who reaches the end of the pool first.
- **Time Tracking**: Calculates the time taken for each swimmer to complete the distance.
- **Real-time & Upload**: Supports both uploaded video files and real-time camera feeds.
